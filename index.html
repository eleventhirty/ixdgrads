About

BDes Hons Interaction Design is a highly creative course aimed at those who aspire for a career as an interaction designer or user experience designer, helping to shape the digital landscape we all, increasingly, live in. The course equips students with the skills they need to meet the challenges of an industry that’s changing rapidly and which offers exciting international career opportunities.

The course is highly rated by industry professionals and benefits from partnerships with world-class companies such as Google, Deloitte Digital, Square, Booking.com, and others. A degree in Interaction Design will offer opportunities in a wide range of exciting and very well-paid roles, including app design and web design; user experience and user interface design; virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR); and digital product design.

Find out more about the course on the Ulster University prospectus for Interaction Design.
